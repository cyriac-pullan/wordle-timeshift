🚀 Wordle: TimeShift Edition

Game Rules

How to Play

  • Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was

Tile Colors

  • 🟩 Green: Letter is in the correct spot
  • 🟨 Yellow: Letter is in the word but wrong spot
  • ⬜ Gray: Letter is not in the word

TimeShift Rules

  • ⏳ Word changes every minute
  • 📝 Keep your guess history
  • 🔄 Previous guesses re-evaluated
  • 🎯 Continue from current guess
  • ⚠️ Game ends after 3 word changes + 1 minute
